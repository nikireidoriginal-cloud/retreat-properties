Why This Model

Economics

  • Centralizing plumbing in one building eliminates the most expensive per-unit cost
  • 1 septic system instead of 3+ saves $20-30K
  • Simple cabins at $12K each vs $55K for a full tiny house
  • Scaling up means adding more $12K cabins as revenue grows
  • Local carpenters can build each cabin in 2-4 weeks

Guest Experience

  • This is the proven retreat model — camps, monasteries, and premium glamping operations all use it
  • Walking to a shared bathhouse is part of the disconnect-and-simplify brand
  • Communal kitchen creates the connection that retreat guests pay for
  • Private sleeping for quiet, shared spaces for community
  • Premium full-bathroom cabins can be added later as a high-margin upgrade
